Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 April 2026

TurboPrep is a training app for high-school Human Powered Vehicle (HPR) athletes preparing for the Victorian HPR Series. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

Plain-English summary: we collect what we need to give you training plans, team leaderboards, race-day tracking and AI coaching. We never sell your data. You can delete your account at any time.

Who we are

TurboPrep is operated by TDOG Industries. Day-to-day support is handled by Tenny Hearn. Contact: hearn.tenny@icloud.com.

Data we collect

Account information

  • Email address — to sign you in and send important account messages
  • Display name — shown to your team and on leaderboards
  • Role (student, coach, parent) — determines what features you see
  • Year level + fitness tier — picks the right training plan

Training data

  • Workouts you log — completion status, duration, perceived effort
  • Race log entries — race results, lap counts, notes
  • Plan progress — which days you've completed, skipped, rescheduled

Health data (only if you grant permission)

  • Heart rate, energy burn, workout duration — read from Apple Health to enrich your training history
  • Health data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up to your TurboPrep account

Location data (only on race day)

  • Coarse location during race-day stints — used to log lap times and verify you were at the track. Not collected outside race day.

Device data (automatic)

  • Push notification token — only if you enable notifications
  • App version + crash logs — to fix bugs (no personal info attached)

How we use your data

  • Render your personalised training plan and progress
  • Show your position on team and global leaderboards
  • Coordinate race-day timing across team members
  • Generate AI coaching insights based on your training history (uses Anthropic's Claude API — see "Third parties" below)
  • Send you opt-in push notifications for workout reminders, race-day alerts, and coach messages
  • Detect and fix bugs (anonymised crash + performance data)

Third parties

We use the following services to run TurboPrep. Each handles data only for the purpose described.

  • Google Firebase (auth, database, hosting) — stores your account and training data. Firebase privacy
  • Apple HealthKit — read-only access to health data on your device, with your explicit permission
  • Anthropic Claude API — generates AI Coach responses. Your training data is sent only when you actively use AI features. Anthropic privacy
  • Strava (optional) — only if you connect your Strava account; rides sync into TurboPrep. Disconnect any time. Strava privacy
  • Netlify (web hosting) and Apple Push Notification service (notifications) — operational infrastructure

Children & minors

TurboPrep is designed for high-school athletes Years 7-12, which includes users aged 12 and over. We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles and Apple's app rules for apps used by minors.

  • We do not show third-party advertising
  • We do not sell or trade personal data
  • Year 7 athletes (typically aged 12-13) should sign up with a parent or guardian's involvement — most schools coordinate this through the HPR team
  • Coaches and parents (with athlete consent) can view athlete progress; athletes control what they share

If you believe a child under 13 has signed up without appropriate consent, email hearn.tenny@icloud.com and we will delete the account.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct — fix anything inaccurate via the in-app Profile tab or by emailing us
  • Delete — close your account and remove your data permanently
  • Disconnect — revoke Strava, Health, or notification permissions any time in app or device settings
  • Export — download your training history in a portable format on request

To exercise any of these rights, email hearn.tenny@icloud.com. We respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we're legally required to keep records (for example, payment receipts).

Data security

Account data is stored in Google Firebase (Australia / global Google datacenters) and protected by Firebase Security Rules so other users can't read your private records. Authentication uses standard Firebase Auth. We use HTTPS for every request.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever suffer a data breach affecting you, we will notify you within 72 hours.

International data transfer

TurboPrep is operated from Australia. Data may be processed in Google's global datacenters (commonly the United States) and Anthropic's processing region (United States). By using TurboPrep you consent to this transfer.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material in this policy, we will notify you in-app and by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Contact

For questions, concerns, or data requests, email hearn.tenny@icloud.com.
