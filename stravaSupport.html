Getting Started

📱 Using VeloForge

VeloForge is a web app — no app store download needed. Open veloforge.netlify.app in your browser, create an account, and add it to your home screen for a native app experience.

On iPhone: tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". On Android: tap the menu → "Add to Home Screen".

Strava Integration

⬡ Connecting Strava

Go to Profile → Strava Integration → "Connect with Strava". You'll be redirected to Strava to authorise VeloForge. Once connected, your recent activities will import automatically.

What VeloForge accesses:

  • Your recent activities (name, type, duration, distance, route, heart rate)
  • Your club memberships (for automatic team grouping)

What VeloForge does NOT access:

  • Your followers, following, or social data
  • Segment data or leaderboards
  • Other athletes' data
  • Your payment or account settings
🔄 Syncing Activities

Activities sync automatically when you open the app. You can also tap "Sync Now" in Profile → Strava to force a sync. Activities recorded in VeloForge's GPS tracker are uploaded to your Strava profile automatically.

🔌 Disconnecting Strava

Go to Profile → Strava Integration → "Disconnect Strava". This immediately revokes VeloForge's access to your Strava account. Your previously imported activities remain in your VeloForge training log, but no new data will be synced.

You can also revoke access directly from Strava Settings → My Apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I connect Strava? ▾
Make sure you're logged into VeloForge (not in Demo Mode). If you see a "403" or "limit exceeded" error, the Strava integration may be pending approval. Contact your coach or the app administrator.
My activities aren't importing ▾
Try Profile → Strava → "Sync Now". If activities still don't appear, disconnect and reconnect Strava. Make sure your activities are set to "Everyone" or "Followers" visibility on Strava (private activities require the activity:read_all scope which VeloForge requests).
Do coaches see my Strava data? ▾
Coaches can see that you've logged workouts (including those imported from Strava), but they see the same summary data as any workout — name, type, duration, and distance. They cannot access your Strava profile, routes, heart rate, or any social data.
Is my Strava data used for AI coaching? ▾
No. VeloForge's AI coaching features explicitly exclude all Strava-sourced data. Only workouts you log manually within VeloForge are included in AI analysis. This complies with Strava's API terms.
I see duplicate activities ▾
This can happen if you record an activity in VeloForge AND it also syncs from Strava. The app has built-in duplicate detection, but if duplicates appear, ask your coach to run the Duplicate Cleanup tool in the Admin panel.
How do I delete my account? ▾
Contact your coach or the app administrator. They can remove your data from the system. Disconnecting Strava first will revoke API access. Your Strava account and data are not affected by deleting your VeloForge account.

Privacy

🔒 Your Data

VeloForge is a school training tool, not a commercial product. Your data is used solely for training tracking and team coordination within your school HPV racing team.

  • Your data is stored in Google Firebase (encrypted at rest)
  • Only you can see your own activity details, routes, and heart rate data
  • Coaches see summary workout counts and streak data for team management
  • Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising
  • Strava data is never processed by AI or machine learning systems
  • You can disconnect Strava and delete your account at any time
